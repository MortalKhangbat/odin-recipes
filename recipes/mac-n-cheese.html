Mac and Cheesy Speical

Special extra cheesy Mac and Cheese

For cheese lovers only, this will rock your cheese taste buds. You will be transported to cheese wonderland. Easy to make but hard to share you are going to want to eat this all by yourself

Ingredients

  • Elbow Pasta
  • Chedder Cheese
  • Monterey Jack
  • Butter
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Salt
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta less than you normally would. I like to boil my elbow pasta for between 4 and 5 minutes.
  2. Pour milk in a baking pan then throw butter and elbow pasta in the pan
  3. Now throw your cheese all over the top
  4. Put bread crumbs over the top and add salt
  5. Bake in the oven at 420 degrees for 15 minutes
  6. Take it out and let it cool for 5 minutes. Then it will be ready to chew up
